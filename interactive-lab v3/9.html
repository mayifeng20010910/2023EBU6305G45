Beihai Park

From： baike.baidu.com

Beihai Park is a classical royal garden with the earliest, the best preserved and the richest cultural deposits in China. The development of Beihai Park, after several dynasties including Jin, Yuan, Ming and Qing dynasties, carries the history and culture of China for nearly one thousand years, and forms the artistic style of garden construction represented by the imperial garden. It's a masterpiece reflecting the great achievements of garden culture and art in the past dynasties.

Around the spring equinox, the peach blossoms next to the White Pagoda on Qionghua Island are in full bloom. The sea of flowers accompanied by a pool setting off the beauty of "Qiongdao Chunyin", one of the eight scenic spots in Beijing.
