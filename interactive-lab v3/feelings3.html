Colors of Beijing

2023-4-17      

Wang Sibo is a light painting artist. More than 10 years ago, he first saw Picasso's light painting in Life Magazine and thought it was amazing! In 2010, Wang Sibo started his own light painting business and has been exploring his own style. As a young man who grew up in Beijing, Wang Sibo likes to paint the beauty of his hometown with light and shadow. For example, he will use light to paint some fun paintings in hutongs. Today, he is creating a work called "Light Painting Beijing," which uses simple light sources to outline Beijing landmarks. Wang Sibo said that in the city where he lives, every brick, every tile and every ancient building has its own story. His idea is simple that is to show the latest, best and most beautiful Beijing to everyone.
