Beijing in the Hutong

2023-4-17      

Hutong, the general name of the north streets or alleys. In Beijing, hutong is synonymous with the city's most distinctive features. Shijia Hutong, one of the earliest hutongs in old Beijing, is home to the country's first community hutong museum. The 21-square-meter historical model of Shijia Hutong in the exhibition hall is a place where every visitor will stop. In addition to the museum, there are also six different micro-gardens in Shijia Hutong, which were transformed from old courtyards by residents and teachers from the Central Academy of Fine Arts, and won the International Landscape Society Award in 2020. In addition, there are Shijia Hutong Cultural Creative Society and Sound of Museum and so on... From the renovation of Shijia Hutong, we can see the love of Hutong residents for their homes and Beijing.
